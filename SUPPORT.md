# Support

## Supported questions

Support covers local package installation, the `chemical-eia` CLI, the Canonical Skill, the Claude Code adapter manifest, fictional minimal examples, archive verification, and interpretation of software-generated validation messages.

When asking for help, provide the released version, operating system, Python version, the exact relative command, the exit code, and a minimal fictional input. Redact user names and machine-specific paths where possible.

## Information not to provide

Do not send real enterprise documents, process recipes, confidential equipment data, personal information, credentials, access tokens, private keys, or proprietary expected results. Support may ask you to replace an attachment with a synthetic reproducer before investigation continues.

## Preview limitation

The v0.1.0 Preview does not provide regulatory conclusions, enterprise confirmation, professional sign-off, or a guarantee that submitted source material is complete. A qualified technician remains responsible for reviewing assumptions, resolving conflicts, recording adoption decisions, and deciding whether results are fit for formal engineering work.

Security-sensitive reports follow `SECURITY.md`, and proposed code or documentation changes follow `CONTRIBUTING.md`.
